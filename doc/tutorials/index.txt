===============
Getting started
===============

Cubane is:

1. For visitors to provide the fastest and highest quality website experience

2. For Owners to provide a very simple and effective way to edit content and

3. For Developers to enable them to deliver the above continuously.

We hope that cubane can make your life as a developer easier by providing you
with a set of tested tools to solve common development challenges in various
business domains.

New to cubane? Walk your way through this material quickly to get up and
running quickly.
